Elders Quorum Home Teaching Report

Things to note:
  • You will use LCR (Leader and Clerk Resources) for much of the Home Teaching Reporting
  • To get to LCR, go to lds.org, click on My Account, click Leader and Clerk Resources and sign in. You can also use this link to get right into LCR: lds.org/lcr
  • Printing: Once you are finished entering all the data for the HT Report, just print like you normally would from the browser (Using Ctrl+P for instance). You should be able to print to PDF instead of a printer. This way you can email the report. (You also probably want to turn off Headers and Footers in the print dialog.)

Backup JSON data

To Back Up: Click in the box below. All the text should automatically be selected. Copy the text to some other location (like a text file).

To Restore Backup: Paste the text you previouscly backed up into the box below and the click the Restore Data button below.

Families not visited by Home Teachers

Though you can certainly edit this section, it is not really needed as long as you are including the list of Families Not Visited.

To get the Families Not Visited along with the Statistics by Month:

  • Go to LCR > Organizations > Home and Visiting Teaching.
  • Make sure the Overview tab is selected.
  • Make sure the Percentages is selected. (On the right side of the page.)
  • Click Print. (A dropdown will appear.)
  • Check Statistics by Month and Households Not visited in 3 Months
  • Click the Print button. (The button below the checkboxes you just checked.)
